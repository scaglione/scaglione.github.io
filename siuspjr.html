The Junior Company of Information Systems also known as SI-USP Jr, is a non-profitable civil association, localized in Sao Paulo at the School of Arts, Sciences and Humanities of the University of Sao Paulo (USP).

The company was founded in the end of 2009 by a small group of students whom was looking for apply phisically the knowledge acquired in the classes and also improve the entrepreneurship into the university. The company grew a lot and currently is singnificant part of the Brazilian Junior Companies Movement. Now a days they have members in many projects in the Technology Area.

My position at the respective company was: Trainee Administrator. I acted as liason between the customers, sponsors and the company, intermediating meetings for example. I also represented the company in some events. I initiated strategies for events and campaigns in partnership with the Marketing team of the company. I detemined: prices, time frame and services included for commercial offers and contracts.
