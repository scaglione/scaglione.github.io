Arraial D'Ajuda / Bahia-Brazil

Paradisiac Beaches and exuberant nature.

One of the best places I have ever been. I cannot wait to go again there. The climate is awesome, for those who like hot weather. The beaches are outstanding, the nature is exuberant and the food is terrific.

This city is located in the Brazilian State of Bahia, and it's a small village with a singular and beautiful colonial architecture. It attracts tourists from all parts of the world and it's also a cultural point hosting a lot of writers, musicians and artists.

The places nearby I would suggest are: Trancoso, Praia da Pitinga , Santa Cruz Cabralia , Praia do Mucuge and Praia de Taipe.
