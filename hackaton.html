Mylan Hackaton

Website: www.mylan.com/hacksummit

Year: 2015

Mylan hosted a Hackaton in its Pittsburgh headquarter. A team of friends and I participated of this Hackaton, which is basically a contest to promote innovative ideas. We came up with an outstading prototype app that manages all the aspects related with a patient's health. Through this app the patient will be able to buy medicines, book appointments with a doctor and remind to take his medicines through medicines that will be automatically set. The prototype version called SnapHealth is available on: Google Play for dowload.

We were awarded first place for the design and idea in the contest among other 30 teams. You can look for more information in our web site: snaphealth.com
